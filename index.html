How to Delegate your Cardano to Dana Node Pool

1 - Open a free account with any online exchange, like Coinbase.com
2 - Buy Cardano (ADA) in your exchange account
3 - Download a Cardano wallet like Yoroi or Daedalus
4 - Transfer a small test amount of Ada from your exchange wallet to your Cardano wallet, verify it was successful, then perform your main transfer
5- In your Yoroi wallet, go to Delegation List, search for DNND , and click delegate, ,
